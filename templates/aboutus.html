Founder’s Note

“My struggle with hair fall became the beginning of something bigger.”

There was a time when my hair fall became uncontrollable. I tried every product available — oils, tonics, serums, and treatments — but nothing worked.

Instead of giving up, I started studying natural ingredients and making small batches at home. After months of trial and error, I created a completely natural formula that finally worked — my hair grew back stronger, healthier, and fuller.

This personal transformation gave birth to Pumpkin Cosmetics. A brand created not out of business, but out of a real struggle, real results, and a desire to help others experiencing the same issues.

The Journey From Hair Loss to Hair Care Innovation

Pumpkin Cosmetics was born from a deeply personal journey. What started as one woman’s attempt to fix her own hair fall soon became a mission to bring natural, powerful, non-toxic haircare to everyone struggling with the same issue.

Every Pumpkin product is carefully researched, hand-formulated, and tested — not on trends, but on real results. The goal is simple: to give you clean beauty solutions that work naturally, gently, and effectively.

Our Core Belief

Beauty should heal, not harm. That’s why Pumpkin focuses on natural, cruelty-free, chemical-free, and gentle formulations that restore confidence and bring real results.
